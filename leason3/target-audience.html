Description

This site has been designed to accommodate owners of small or medium type businesses that are thinking of expanding their business into the community. Such business owners that I have taken into consideration of are supermarket, mall, and wholesale business owners. In addition, its purpose is to connect these business owners to have more versatility in the community in terms of collaboration.
